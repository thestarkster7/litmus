About the club

Litmus is the Debating, Writing and MUN Club of Manipal University Jaipur. With regular activities, discussions, events and meetings, Litmus is one of the most thriving cultural clubs of MUJ. The spirit of this club revolves around providing students open grounds for discussion, debate, growth and learning. Apart from literary activities, Litmus hosts fun events that excite and challenge the brain simultaneously. Litmus is a home to literature, debating and MUN enthusiasts. Anyone with an opinion and a penchant for expressing it finds their place here among the vast diversity of writers, and debaters.

The club hosts events all year round, ranging from creative writing competitions, several forms of debate competitions to mock MUNs and workshops. Litmus also hosts the annual Manipal University Jaipur Model United Nations, one of the most awaited events each year. The club also regularly updates their members on upcoming opportunities to participate in and provides literary resources, recommendations and a platform to exhibit one's calibre. Litmus is the place where one can not only find their voice, but also make it heard. This is where the thinking hats are.
